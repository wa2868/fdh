Story:

In a world of powerful gods and warring mortals, two enchanted beings meet in a peace-filled realm. Su Mo (Li Yi Feng), a prince of sea gods, and Bai Ying (Chen Yu Qi), a princess descended from a mighty sword deity, are immediately drawn to each other. Their romance, however, sparks animosity between two factions, angering the gods. Su Mo is eventually expelled from the realm, while Bai Ying, overwhelmed with grief, tries to end her life by jumping from a pagoda.

Meanwhile, Su Mo, now the sea emperor, embarks on a search for Bai Ying. As battles rage in multiple realms, the future of both worlds is at stake. Can they save their worlds and reunite in love?
