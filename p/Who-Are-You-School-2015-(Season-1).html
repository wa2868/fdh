Story:

Lee Eun Bi, an orphan girl, is severely bullied at school. Her identical twin, Go Eun Byul, is a popular girl in the prestigious Sekang High School. After Eun Byul mysteriously disappears, Eun Bi loses her memory and is mistaken for her twin.
