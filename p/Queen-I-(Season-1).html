Story:

Kim Boong-Do (Ji Hyun-Woo) is a nobleman from the Joseon Dynasty, living approximately 300 years ago. By a twist of fate, he travels through time and finds himself in modern-day Seoul, South Korea. There, he meets Choi Hee-Jin (Yoo In-Na), a struggling actress who is portraying Queen In-Hyun in the drama series "New Jang Hee-Bin". As their paths intertwine, these two unlikely individuals find themselves falling into a romantic relationship that transcends time and space.
